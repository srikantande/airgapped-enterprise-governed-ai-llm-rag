2. GENERAL WORKPLACE POLICIES
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Friday (40 hours per week). Flexible timings can be availed with 
  managerial approval.
* Leave Policy:
  - Earned Leave (EL): 15 days per annum.
  - Casual Leave (CL): 7 days per annum.
  - Sick Leave (SL): 7 days per annum.
  - Public Holidays: 10 fixed mandatory and national holidays, plus 2 
    optional/restricted holidays.


3. POLICIES & LABOUR LAWS BIFURCATED BY GENDER (As per Indian Law)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. POLICIES FOR FEMALE EMPLOYEES:
1. Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2017: 
   - Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for 
     the first two children (and 12 weeks for subsequent children).
   - Provisions for 'Work from Home' post-maternity leave, subject to the 
     nature of work and managerial approval.
   - Crèche facility access is provided as the company strength exceeds 
     50 employees.
2. The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (POSH) Act, 2013:
   - SriLab.AI maintains a strict Zero-Tolerance policy against harassment.
   - An Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) headed by a senior female 
     employee has been constituted to address and resolve any grievances.
3. Safety During Night Shifts (State Shops & Establishments Act):
   - Female employees working between 8:00 PM and 6:00 AM will be provided 
     with mandatory company-arranged secure transportation (door-to-door).

B. POLICIES FOR MALE EMPLOYEES:
1. Paternity Leave (Company Policy aligned with progressive standards):
   - Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks (14 calendar days) of paid 
     paternity leave to bond with their newborn or adopted child.
2. Equal Opportunity & Anti-Harassment:
   - While POSH legally focuses on women, SriLab.AI enforces a gender-neutral 
     anti-harassment workplace. Any complaints of harassment from male 
     employees are handled with equal severity by the HR Disciplinary Committee.

C. COMMON STATUTORY COMPLIANCES FOR ALL EMPLOYEES:
1. Equal Remuneration Act, 1976: Complete parity in wages for male and female 
   employees performing the same or similar work.
2. Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952.
3. Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 (Payable after 5 years of continuous service).


4. COMPENSATION BREAKDOWN & CTC POLICY (Compliant with New Wage Codes)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
SriLab.AI's compensation structure is strictly aligned with the latest Indian 
Labour Laws, specifically the New Code on Wages. Under these codes, the "Wages" 
(Basic Salary) must constitute at least 50% of the Gross Remuneration.

A. UNIVERSAL CALCULATION FORMULAS (For any Fixed CTC)
You can calculate your exact salary components based on your Total Fixed CTC 
using the standardized formulas below. 

Step 1: Determine Gross Salary
* Gross Salary = Total Fixed CTC ÷ 1.0841

Step 2: Calculate Individual Components
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Component                   | Formula (Based on Gross Salary / Basic) | Approx % of Total CTC |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Basic Salary                | 50% of Gross Salary                     | ~46.12%               |
| House Rent Allowance (HRA)  | 50% of Basic Salary (Metro)             | ~23.06%               |
| Leave Travel Allowance (LTA)| 10% of Basic Salary                     | ~ 4.61%               |
| Special Allowance           | Gross Salary - (Basic + HRA + LTA)      | ~18.45%               |
| Employer PF (Retiral)       | 12% of Basic Salary                     | ~ 5.53%               |
| Gratuity (Retiral)          | 4.81% of Basic Salary (15/26/12)        | ~ 2.22%               |
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
*Note: Total CTC = Gross Salary + Employer PF + Gratuity*
